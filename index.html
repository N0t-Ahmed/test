Why Use Solar Panels

We are going to talk about how beneficial and how solar panels work

Solar radiation is light, that is also known as electromagnetic radiation, that is emitted by the sun. While every location on Earth receives some sunlight over a year, the amount of solar radiation that reaches anywhere on the Earth's surface varies. Solar technologies capture this radiation and turn it into useful forms of energy. Saving natural resources, and can help prevent global warming.
